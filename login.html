Home

Now that you are logged in, navigate to the New Trip page and create your first trip by entering your location along with your friend's and hitting the submit button. The three main cities within the midpoint of your two locations will then populate in the table along with a total price of the cheapest airline tickets for both you and your friend. Decide which destination looks most appealing and click the corresponding save button to save your trip to the View Trips page.
